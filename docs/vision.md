# TaskFlow Vision

## Product Vision

TaskFlow is a workflow management application designed for small teams (2–10 members).

The goal is to help every team member stay focused on the most important work, understand workload distribution, and keep projects moving without unnecessary complexity.

---

## Target Users

Small teams with 2–10 members.

Examples:

Software development teams
Design teams
Creative agencies
Small business teams

---

## Problems We Solve

Miscommunication about responsibilities
Uneven workload distribution
Too many unnecessary project management features
Difficulty identifying blocked work
Lack of visibility into project health

---

## Core Principles

1. Focus First
2. Team Awareness
3. Less is Better
4. Visual Workflow
5. Collaboration Without Noise

---

## Core Features (MVP)

Kanban Board
Task Management
Priority Levels
Workload Overview
Mentions (@username)
Project Health Indicators

---

## Out of Scope

TaskFlow will NOT include:

General Chat
Voice Call
Video Meeting
Time Tracking
Payroll
Invoice
Financial Management

---

## Product Goal

A team should be able to open TaskFlow and immediately know:

What should I work on today?
Who needs help?
Which project needs attention?
